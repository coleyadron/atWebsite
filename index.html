About the Artist

August Thompson is an aspiring comic illustrator and writer with a lifelong passion for superheroes. Inspired by the movies, television shows, and comic books that shaped his childhood, August strives to bring that same sense of wonder and excitement to his own work. His goal is to create dynamic, character-driven narratives that explore human imperfections, making his stories not only thrilling but deeply relatable. Ultimately, he is a storyteller, dedicated to blending compelling narratives with striking visuals to leave a lasting impact on readers.
